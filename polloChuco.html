Pollo chuco

Description

Pollo chuco is a recipe originated in Honduras that involves deep fried chicken, diferent sauces, plantain chips and encurtido which is a garnish.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • ketchup
  • mayonnaise
  • plantain
  • cabbage

Steps

  1. fry chicken
  2. fry plantain chips
  3. prepare sauces
  4. boil cabbage
  5. assemble and garnish chicken and chips with lots of suace, onion and cabbage
